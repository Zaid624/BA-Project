# Resume Bullets

- Analyzed 9,977 cleaned retail transaction lines to identify discount, regional, and category-level margin drivers across $2.30M in sales and $286K in profit.
- Built SQL/Python analysis outputs and a Streamlit dashboard showing that discounts above 20% were collectively loss-making, with the 60%+ discount band losing $70.6K at a -122.63% margin.
- Identified Furniture as a margin-recovery priority, generating $741.3K in sales but only $18.4K in profit at a 2.49% margin versus Technology at 17.40%.
- Produced stakeholder-ready English and German deliverables, Power BI-ready CSV exports, and data-quality tests documenting 17 exact duplicate records removed from a 9,994-row source table.
