The Head of Sales has commissioned this analysis to understand why sales growth does not translate consistently into profit across regions, categories, and discount levels. The core business problem is to identify where discounting and product mix are supporting profitable growth, and where they are eroding margin despite generating revenue. The analysis should inform decisions on regional sales focus, category-level pricing discipline, and which discount bands require tighter commercial rules. The work is based on the available Superstore transaction fields, including region, state, city, segment, category, sub-category, sales, quantity, discount, and profit. Out of scope are causal claims about discount effectiveness, customer lifetime value, inventory constraints, supplier costs, and time-series seasonality, because the current dataset does not include order dates, customer identifiers, product names, cost-of-goods detail, or campaign history.
